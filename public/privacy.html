Privacy Policy for SreeCalc

Effective Date: December 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains how SreeCalc handles your information.

1. No Data Collection

SreeCalc is a standard scientific calculator tool. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, location data, or usage statistics.

2. Offline Functionality

All math calculations are performed locally on your own device. No data is sent to any servers.

3. Third Parties

This app does not use any third-party advertising or analytics services that track you.

If you have questions, please contact the developer through the GitHub repository.
